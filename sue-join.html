How to Invite and Install Sue Bot

Sue Bot - Trilobite Tavern Discord Server

Welcome! If you're looking to invite and install the Sue bot in your server, please follow the steps below:

1. Join the Trilobite Tavern Discord Server

First, you need to join the Trilobite Tavern Discord server and agree to the server rules.

2. Read and Agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

Next, you need to read, understand, acknowledge, and agree to both the Terms of Service and the Privacy Policy of the Sue bot.

3. Create a Support Ticket

Once you have agreed to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, create a support ticket in the Trilobite Tavern team. Through this support ticket, request access to the Sue bot.

Important Information

Remember, installation and configuration of the Sue bot can only be done by the bot developer, Tyzeron. Tyzeron has the right to modify the bot or revoke access at any point in time without any prior warning.
